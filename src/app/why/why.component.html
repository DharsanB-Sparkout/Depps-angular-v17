WHY

Food Safety & Quality

Traceability allows the agricultural industry to track food from "farm to fork, ensuring consumers get safe, high-quality products by identifying contamination sources quickly.

Transparency & Trust

By tracking movement through the supply chain, everyone involved gains visibility, building trust with consumers who increasingly demand transparency in their food choices

DePPs empower small farmers

DePPs ensure ALL food, regardless of brand, has a trackable "passport" for origin & and quality

Transparency wins, not labels.
